Synthetic assets are decentralized finance (DeFi) analogues of derivatives in the traditional finance world. This post breaks down the key concepts from our systematization of knowledge paper.

## What Are Synthetic Assets?

Financial arrangements which derive value from and are directly pegged to fluctuations in the value of an underlying asset.

## Why They Matter

Synthetic assets occupy a unique niche, serving to facilitate currency exchange, giving traders a means to speculate on the value of crypto assets without directly holding them.

## Risks and Considerations

The academic literature on this topic is highly disparate and struggles to keep up with rapid changes in the space.
